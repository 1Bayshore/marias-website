About Maria's Pizza

Maria's Pizza is a business delivering pizza and snacks to classrooms every friday. Parents order online, and pizza is delivered to students. Pizza is currently delivered from Belmont Pizza, and snacks from UNFI (unfi.org). For more information email or call the pizza managers.
